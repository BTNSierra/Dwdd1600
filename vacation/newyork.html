View What New York City Has To Offer

New York City is a vibrant, exciting destination that offers something for everyone. From iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park to world-class museums, Broadway shows, and diverse dining experiences, there's always something new and exciting to explore. The city's unique energy is contagious, and no matter what your interests are, you're sure to find something that captivates your imagination. Whether you're a history buff, a foodie, an art lover, or just looking for an unforgettable adventure, New York City has it all. The city's rich cultural diversity and bustling atmosphere make it an ideal destination for anyone seeking a memorable and rewarding vacation experience.

More About The Blakely by LuxUrban

Well located in the center of New York, The Blakely by LuxUrban is within a 4-minute walk of Broadway Theatre and 400 yards of Museum of Modern Art. The property is close to several well-known attractions, a 9-minute walk from Top of the Rock, half a mile from St Patrick's Cathedral and a 11-minute walk from Central Park. The hotel provides a terrace, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a microwave, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower. At The Blakely by LuxUrban each room has bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Radio City Music Hall, Carnegie Hall and Rockefeller Center. The nearest airport is LaGuardia Airport, 6.8 miles from The Blakely by LuxUrban. Families in particular like the location – they rated 9.5 for a stay with kids.

More About The Manhattan Club

Located 1 block from Carnegie Hall and 3 blocks from Central Park, this all-suite hotel features a private club lounge. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. A flat-screen TV, marble bathroom and kitchenette are found in each modern suite of The Manhattan Club. A coffee maker, work desk and ironing facilities are included. Guests can work out in the large fitness room with TVs and a changing area. A business center and valet parking are also available. Breakfast is served in the Club Manhattan private lounge daily. At night, the lounge turns into an intimate setting for cocktails. The 57th Street/7th Avenue subway station is located next to the hotel. The Museum of Modern Art is an 8-minute walk away. Families in particular like the location – they rated 9.6 for a stay with kids.

More About Aura Hotel

Aura Hotel Times Square is located 531 feet from Times Square and 2113 feet from Rockefeller Center. All rooms at this nonsmoking property feature a flat-screen TV, bathrobes and complimentary toiletries. Guests at Aura Hotel can enjoy concierge service, 24-hour front desk, a fitness center and a bar. Other amenities include a business center and dry cleaning services. Bryant Park is 1056 feet from the property and Madame Tussauds is 1585 feet away. Empire State Building is 0.8 mi away. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.4 for a two-person trip.
