More About Imperial York Street Apartments

Britannia International Hotel Canary Wharf has spacious rooms and limited free WiFi, just 1969 feet from Canary Wharf Underground Station. The traditional rooms at Britannia International Canary Wharf have private bathrooms, some with spa baths. All rooms have tea/coffee making facilities and a work desk. Many have Docklands views. Jenny’s Restaurant serves traditional British cuisine and cooked breakfasts. The Pizzeria offers Italian cuisine, pizza's, pasta, grills and burgers. The Hotel Britannia International Canary Wharf is a 10-minute drive from the O2 Arena, and Millwall is 15 minutes’ walk away. IFS Cloud Cable car is nearby. Greenwich can be reached in 15 minutes’ drive, and on-site parking is available. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.1 for a two-person trip.

More About Zedwell Piccadilly Circus

Located within a few steps of Prince Of Wales Theatre and a 7-minute walk of Trafalgar Square, Zedwell Piccadilly Circus has rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in London. The property is close to several well-known attractions, a 6-minute walk from Arts Theatre, 600 yards from National Gallery London and a 6-minute walk from Carnaby Street. The property is 400 yards from the city center and a 2-minute walk from Queen's Theatre. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at the hotel also have free WiFi, while some rooms also offer a city view. At Zedwell Piccadilly Circus all rooms have bed linen and towels. An à la carte, Full English/Irish or vegetarian breakfast is available every morning at the property. The accommodation can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area. Popular points of interest near Zedwell Piccadilly Circus include Prince Edward Theatre, Piccadilly Circus and Leicester Square Tube Station. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 8.7 miles from the hotel. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.6 for a two-person trip.
