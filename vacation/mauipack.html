More About Kahana Falls Resort

Located across from Kahana Beach, this resort offers free WiFi and a swimming pool. A 24-hour reception makes guest check-in convenient. Cable TV and air conditioning are in each room at the Kahana Falls Resort. Select rooms have a full kitchen and patio. Ceiling fans are also in the rooms for guest comfort. Paradise Excursions are on site at the Kahana Falls Resort for guests to book activities during their stay. Waterfalls and a sandy-bottomed hot tub are also on site. Lounge chairs are available alongside the naturally landscaped swimming pool. Barbecue facilities, a fitness center and launderette are also available for all guests Lahaina Entertainment District is 10 miles away from the resort. Kahului Airport is 30 miles away and Kaanapali Golf Course is 4 miles away. The smaller Kapalua Airport is 1 mile away. Families in particular like the location – they rated 9.0 for a stay with kids.

Most Popular Amenities

Outdoor swimming pool• Free parking• Non-smoking rooms• Free Wifi• Fitness center• Family rooms

More About Napili Village Hotel

Offering pool views, Napili Village Hotel in Kahana features accommodations, a garden, and a terrace. With sea views, this accommodation provides a balcony and a swimming pool. The condo hotel offers rooms with air conditioning, free private parking, and free Wifi. All units comprise a seating area with a sofa, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen with various cooking facilities, including an oven, a microwave, a toaster, and a fridge. There's a private bathroom with shower in all units, along with a hair dryer and free toiletries. At the condo hotel, each unit comes with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the condo hotel include Napili Beach, Kapalua Beach, and Oneloa Bay Beach. The nearest airport is Kapalua Airport, 2.5 miles from Napili Village Hotel. Families in particular like the location – they rated 9.1 for a stay with kids.

Most Popular Amenities

Outdoor swimming pool• Free parking• Non-smoking rooms• Free Wifi

More About Outrigger Kāʻanapali Beach Resort

Recognized by the Waiaha Foundation as "Hawaii's Most Hawaiian Hotel," OUTRIGGER Kāʻanapali Beach Resort sits on 11 acres and offers cultural activities and comfortable guestrooms. This hotel also offers free WiFi and no resort fee. Ideally placed on Ka'anapali Beach in Maui, views of the ocean, garden, and pool are offered with each room at this resort. Guests can enjoy the comforts of air conditioning as well as a flat-screen cable TV that offers in-room entertainment. The private bathroom comes equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer. A refrigerator is also available for guest convenience. Located among lush tropical gardens and seconds from pristine beaches, the OUTRIGGER Kāʻanapali Beach Resort offers an ideal location for relaxation. Guests at the resort will also find easy access to numerous recreational activities, including whale watching, surfing and snorkeling. While staying at the Ka'anapali Hotel, guests can enjoy daily hula shows as well as dinner theater performances and cultural activities, including lei making. The resort also features on-site restaurants as well as a spacious outdoor pool. Whalers Village is just 3 minutes' walk from the resort. Kahului Airport is 28 mi away. Families in particular like the location – they rated 9.3 for a stay with kids.
