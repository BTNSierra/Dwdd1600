View What Paris Has To Offer

Paris is a captivating destination that offers a perfect blend of art, history, cuisine, and romance. With its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral, Paris offers a feast for the eyes and the soul. The city's charming streets lined with quaint cafes and fashion boutiques provide a delightful experience for any visitor. Additionally, Paris is renowned for its world-class cuisine, from delectable pastries to gourmet dining experiences. Whether you're strolling along the Seine River, exploring the eclectic neighborhoods, or admiring the timeless architecture, Paris never fails to enchant travelers with its timeless beauty and cultural treasures.

More About Hôtel Vendome Saint-Germain

Hôtel Vendôme Saint-Germain offers accommodations in Paris just 5 minutes' walk from the Panthéon and 2297 feet from Noter Dame Cathedral, featuring free WiFi throughout the property and a 24-hour front desk. Cardinal Lemoine Metro Station is within a minute's walk of the hotel. Each room is fitted with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Extra features include a kettle, fridge and safe in the room. For your comfort, a private bathroom with a hairdryer is available. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at Hôtel Vendome Saint-Germain. Sorbonne University is 2133 feet from Hôtel Vendôme Saint-Germain, while Pompidou Center is 0.9 mi from the property. Paris - Orly Airport is 8.1 mi away. The Seine River is only a 3-minute walk away, offering guests chance to easily access many of the city's sights. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.3 for a two-person trip.

More About Green Park Hotel

Featuring a bar, GREEN PARK HOTEL PARIS is located in Paris in the Île de France region, 2 miles from Gare de l'Est and 2.3 miles from Gare du Nord. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. At the hotel, rooms include a closet. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hairdryer, certain units at GREEN PARK HOTEL PARIS also provide guests with a city view. At the accommodation, every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box. Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, Full English/Irish and Asian options. La Cigale Concert Hall is 2.6 miles from GREEN PARK HOTEL PARIS, while Pompidou Center is 2.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Paris - Charles De Gaulle Airport, 11 miles from the hotel. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.

More About Les Rives Oceanik

Conveniently set in the center of Paris, Les Rives Oceanik provides continental breakfast and free WiFi throughout the property. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service. Guests can use a bar. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a coffee machine, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. Les Rives Oceanik has certain units with city views, and all rooms are equipped with an electric tea pot. At the accommodation every room comes with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near Les Rives Oceanik include Luxembourg Gardens, Sainte Chapelle and Notre Dame. The nearest airport is Paris - Orly Airport, 9.3 miles from the hotel.
