View What Rome Has To Offer

Rome is a captivating blend of ancient wonders, vibrant street life, and rich culinary traditions that make it a must-visit destination for travelers. The city's history comes alive through its iconic landmarks such as the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and the Pantheon, offering a fascinating glimpse into the glory of the Roman Empire. The charming cobblestone streets lead to quaint piazzas where locals and visitors gather to enjoy the city's legendary gelato and espresso. Rome's art and culture scene is unparalleled, with renowned museums and art galleries showcasing masterpieces by the likes of Michelangelo and Bernini. Whether exploring its architectural marvels, indulging in delectable Italian cuisine, or simply soaking in the city's timeless charm, Rome offers a truly unforgettable experience for every traveler.

More About Barcaccia Luxury Suites

Barcaccia Luxury Suites provides rooms in Rome near Barberini Metro Station and Piazza Barberini. Among the facilities at this property are luggage storage space and daily room service, along with free Wifi throughout the property. The property is half a mile from the city center and a few steps from Via Condotti. Accessible via private entrance, all rooms have air conditioning, soundproofing, wardrobe, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Guests can have wine or champagne and chocolates or cookies delivered to their room. At the guest house, every unit includes bed linen and towels. A mini-market is available at the guest house. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Piazza di Spagna, Spanish Steps, and Trevi Fountain. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 9.3 miles from Barcaccia Luxury Suites, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.9 for a two-person trip.

More About Relais Virgilio

Featuring city views, Relais Virgilio in Rome features accommodations and a shared lounge. Among the facilities at this property are an elevator and daily room service, along with free Wifi throughout the property. The property is a 5-minute walk from Campo de' Fiori, and within 0.7 miles of the city center. The guest house will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a desk, a coffee machine, a fridge, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Guests can have chocolates or cookies delivered to their room. At the guest house, units include bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Torre Argentina, Piazza Navona, and Pantheon. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 11 miles from Relais Virgilio. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.7 for a two-person trip.

More About Parisii Bocca di Leone 88

Parisii Bocca di Leone 88 is a recently renovated bed and breakfast in the center of Rome, a 4-minute walk from Spanish Steps and 0.4 miles from Trevi Fountain. The property is close to Piazza di Spagna Metro Station, Via Condotti, and Piazza di Spagna. The accommodation offers an elevator, luggage storage space, and organizing tours for guests. The bed and breakfast will provide guests with air-conditioned units offering a desk, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. Free Wifi is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms also offer a terrace. At the bed and breakfast, units are equipped with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the bed and breakfast include Barberini Metro Station, Piazza Barberini, and Quirinale Palace. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 9.3 miles from Parisii Bocca di Leone 88, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.9 for a two-person trip.
